Introduction

Welcome to FocusFlow. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

By using FocusFlow, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you have any questions, please contact us at privacy@focusflow.world

📋

Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use FocusFlow:

  • Name and email address when you create an account
  • Password (stored as an encrypted hash — we never see your actual password)
  • Tasks, goals, and descriptions you create in the app
  • Completion photos you choose to attach to tasks
  • Subscription and purchase information via Google Play
  • App usage data such as tasks completed and weekly stats

We do not collect your location, contacts, microphone, or any data unrelated to app functionality.

⚙️

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your FocusFlow account
  • Generate AI-powered task plans based on your goals
  • Send weekly progress reports and AI insights
  • Send password reset emails when requested
  • Process your subscription through Google Play
  • Improve the app features and user experience
  • Respond to your support requests
🤖

AI Features & Claude AI

FocusFlow uses Anthropic Claude AI to generate personalised task plans, refine your goals through chat, and produce weekly progress reports.

When you use AI features, your goal descriptions and task data are sent to Anthropic's API to generate responses. This data is used only to generate your plan and is not stored by Anthropic beyond what is necessary to process your request.

AI features are only available on the Pro plan. Free users do not have their data sent to AI services.

🔒

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on Railway.app servers using a PostgreSQL database with industry-standard encryption.

  • Passwords are hashed using BCrypt encryption
  • All API communication uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • JWT tokens are used for secure authentication
  • Database access is restricted to the application only

We implement reasonable security measures but no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

📸

Completion Photos

When you choose to take or upload a completion photo for a task, that photo is stored securely on our servers and is only visible to you in your account.

  • Photos are entirely optional — you are never required to take one
  • Photos are only visible to you — not to other users
  • You can download your photos at any time
  • Photos are deleted when you delete your account
🤝

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We share data only with the following trusted service providers:

  • Anthropic — Claude AI for generating task plans (Pro users only)
  • Railway.app — Backend hosting and database services
  • Google Play — Subscription and payment processing
  • Resend — Transactional email delivery (password resets)
  • RevenueCat — Subscription management across platforms
💳

Subscriptions & Payments

FocusFlow offers a free plan and a Pro subscription at ₹249/month processed through Google Play Store.

  • All payments are processed by Google Play — we never see your card details
  • Subscriptions auto-renew unless cancelled before the renewal date
  • You can cancel anytime through Google Play subscriptions settings
  • Refunds are handled according to Google Play's refund policy
✅

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion — request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Portability — request your data in a portable format
  • Objection — object to processing of your personal data

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@focusflow.world and we will respond within 30 days.
